About Me

Hello, my name is Mishelle Marina and I am a Junior Programmer.

After years of working in fields that didn't appeal to me, I decided to make a significant change in my life to pursue something I truly enjoy. For the past couple of years, I have been training in the world of programming. I have completed several courses at various universities, including some in English-speaking countries, and I am currently enrolled in a formal education program, specifically a Higher Degree in Multiplatform Applications.

I am passionate about solving complex problems and creating efficient, scalable solutions. I have worked on several projects as part of my studies, which are detailed in the portfolio. My strength lies in back-end development, particularly with Python, although I am also capable of working as a full stack developer. I am proficient in Python and SQL, and have experience with frameworks such as Django and Bootstrap.

I am looking for a position where I can apply all my training and skills, and continue to grow as a developer.

WoodShop

WoodShop_Java is a console-based Java application developed in Java 23, designed to efficiently manage wood product stores. It supports full management of products, inventory, shops, and clients, including anonymous, professional, and loyalty members. The system enables detailed ticket generation with automatic discount application for professional clients, ticket filtering by date range, and a dynamic data loading system. It also includes a sales summary per store between dates and input validation to ensure chronological correctness. Key highlights include clean and modular code using object-oriented programming, customized input validation, and a clear separation of concerns between product, inventory, client, and sales management.


Project Details

Technologies: Java 23, IntelliJ IDEA

Completed: April 2025
